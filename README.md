# Rootline

**Verify what you eat. Prove what you sell.**

Rootline is a mobile-first, hardware-free farm-to-table traceability platform that connects every participant in a food supply chain — from the farm to the shelf — through a lightweight digital audit trail. Retailers gain live supply chain visibility and FSMA 204 compliance. Consumers get a scannable QR code that shows the verified journey of their food in under 30 seconds, at the moment of purchase.

---

## The Problem

Premium food retailers charge 10–25% more for organic, sustainably sourced, cold-chain-verified products. But the supply chain data that would actually verify these claims — across 8 independent parties from farm to shelf — exists today as WhatsApp photos, handwritten temperature logs, and emailed PDFs.

That gap between claim and proof costs retailers consumer trust, competitive differentiation, and — as FDA FSMA Section 204 enforcement approaches in July 2028 — regulatory standing.

---

## What Rootline Does

**For supply chain participants (farmers, cold storage, transporters):**
A mobile web portal — no app download, no hardware — where any participant logs a photo-verified handoff record in under 60 seconds. Works offline; syncs automatically when connectivity returns.

**For retailers:**
A live dashboard showing the status of every active product lot across the full supplier network. Automated alerts for cold chain deviations and overdue handoffs. One-click FSMA 204 audit export.

**For consumers:**
A white-labeled QR experience — displayed under the retailer's brand — showing a clean, verified provenance timeline: farm name and photo, organic certification badge, cold chain status, and date of arrival at their store.
